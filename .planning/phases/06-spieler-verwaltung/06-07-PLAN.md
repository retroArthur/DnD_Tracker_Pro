<objective>
Gap closure for UAT finding `skills-layout` (06-HUMAN-UAT.md): the Fertigkeiten section in the
character detail modal looked "zu verteilt und ungerade" — the 5 attribute groups (STR=1, DEX=3,
INT=5, WIS=5, CHA=4 skills; CON has none) were laid out with `display: grid;
grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(150px, 1fr))`, which stretched groups across the
full modal width and placed them row-by-row — producing wide name↔modifier gaps and orphaning the
CHA group onto its own row with empty cells beside it.

Reworked to a balanced masonry-style multi-column flow so the uneven groups pack densely and balance
by height, with compact rows.
</objective>
